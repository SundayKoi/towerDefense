║  TURRETS  (14 total — 3 starters + 11 unlocked across acts 1–4)           ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

─── DPS / FRONTLINE ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[FIREWALL]                          starter
  damage: 14   range: 2.4   rate: 1.3/s   type: KINETIC
  Kinetic rounds. Reliable frontline, cheap to field.
  → Every run starts with a FIREWALL deploy token.

[ANTIVIRUS]                         starter
  damage: 60   range: 3.8   rate: 0.55/s   type: PIERCE
  Long-range pierce sniper. Cracks armor with precision.

[QUANTUM]                           unlock: Act 1 Map 2 (NEXUS HUB) — easy
  damage: 24   range: 2.8   rate: 1.4/s   type: ENERGY
  35% crit chance, 3.5× crit damage. Crits ignore phase resistance.

[CHAIN LIGHTNING]                   unlock: Act 1 Map 3 (MAINFRAME) — easy
  damage: 42   range: 2.5   rate: 0.75/s   type: CHAIN
  Arcs through 3 nearby enemies, 0.8 falloff per jump.
  ⚠ Useless against CORRUPTOR (chain-immune).

[ICE-BREAKER]                       unlock: Act 1 Map 4 (DATA LAKE) — easy
  damage: 90   range: 2.8   rate: 0.4/s   type: AOE
  AoE shredder, 0.95 explosion radius. Reveals stealth, shreds swarms.

[ARTILLERY]                         unlock: Act 2 Map 8 (INFERNET) — medium
  damage: 60   range: 5.5   rate: 0.55/s   type: AOE
  Heavy artillery — slow firing, massive 1.6 AoE radius.
  Lobs high-arc shells. Best against dense groups.

[OVERWATCH] (sniper)                unlock: Act 3 Map 9 (HARDLOCK) — easy
  damage: 380   range: 6.5   rate: 0.18/s   type: PIERCE
  Extreme range, massive damage, 40% crit at 4×. Single-target precision.

[RAILGUN]                           unlock: Act 3 Map 11 (BASTION) — easy
  damage: 220   range: 5.0   rate: 0.25/s   type: PIERCE
  Pierces every enemy in a line. Ignores most armor.

─── SUPPORT / CONTROL ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[HONEYPOT]                          starter
  damage: 4   range: 2.0   rate: 1.8/s   type: ENERGY
  Slows 45% for 1.8s. Low damage, critical for crowd control.
  Drops sticky goo puddles on hit.

[EMP ARRAY] (pulse)                 unlock: Act 2 Map 7 (ORBITAL) — easy
  damage: 55   range: 2.2   rate: 1 burst/2.5s   type: ENERGY
  Radial EM burst every 2.5s. Hits every enemy in range simultaneously.

[DISRUPTOR] (scrambler)             unlock: Act 2 Map 5 (NEON DISTRICT) — easy
  damage: 10   range: 2.4   rate: 2.8/s   type: ENERGY
  Rapid bolts strip 3 armor per hit for 3s.
  Enables lethal combos with other turrets.

[SENTINEL NODE]                     unlock: Act 3 Map 12 (KEEPSAKE) — medium
  damage: 12/sec   range: 2.8   passive field   type: ENERGY
  Passive field — 12 dmg/s and 20% slow to all enemies in range.
  No projectiles.

─── PASSIVE / AURA ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[BOOSTER NODE]                      unlock: Act 1 Map 1 (GRID.01) — easy
  damage: 0   range: 1.5   passive support
  Adjacent turrets gain +25% damage and +15% fire rate. No attack.

[DECRYPT NODE] (data_miner)         unlock: Act 4 Map 13 (GLITCHWIRE) — easy
  damage: 0   range: 2.0   passive aura
  Enemies inside aura take +15% damage from ALL sources. No combat.


╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║  ENEMIES  (18 total — 12 standard + 6 bosses)                             ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

─── LOW THREAT (cannon fodder) ───────────────────────────────────────────────

[WORM]
  hp: 28   speed: 1.5   damage: 1   no armor
  Basic self-replicating probe. Common swarm unit.
  Counter: anything kills worms. Economy matters more than tower choice.

[CRAWLER] (spider)
  hp: 16   speed: 2.8 (FAST)   damage: 1   no armor
  Fast scuttling crawler. Low HP, may slip past sniper turrets.
  Counter: fast-firing FIREWALL or AOE handles swarms; ANTIVIRUS struggles.

─── MEDIUM THREAT ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[TROJAN]
  hp: 70   speed: 1.1   damage: 2   armor: 2
  resist: kinetic 0.65×   weak: pierce 1.3×
  Armored payload carrier. Slow but durable.
  Counter: PIERCE weapons bypass armor. Kinetic struggles.

[LEECH]                              ★ priority kill
  hp: 40   speed: 1.4   damage: 1   no armor
  resist: energy 0.8×
  Heals enemies behind it continuously. Renderer marks with magenta ring + tether.
  Counter: priority kill. HONEYPOT slows them for easy pickoff.

[GLITCH]
  hp: 55   speed: 1.6   damage: 2
  weak: aoe 1.3×
  Corrupted packet. Splits into 2 worms on death — kill with AoE before the split.
  Counter: ICE-BREAKER or PULSE kills it before split. Single-target wastes a shot.

─── HIGH THREAT ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[ROOTKIT]
  hp: 180   speed: 0.85   damage: 3   armor: 5
  resist: kinetic 0.45×, aoe 0.75×   weak: pierce 1.5×
  Root-level intrusion. Extremely armored.
  Counter: RAILGUN/ANTIVIRUS pierce shreds them. Kinetic wastes DPS.

[PHANTOM]
  hp: 45   speed: 2.0   damage: 2
  resist: kinetic 0.5×, pierce 0.6×   weak: energy 1.3×
  CRIT IGNORES RESIST. Phases out of reality. Kinetic half-misses.
  Counter: QUANTUM crits ignore resistance. ENERGY is strong.

[BOMBER]                             ⚠ low-HP red pulse telegraph
  hp: 60   speed: 1.3   damage: 5   no armor
  resist: aoe 0.5×
  Detonates on death. Devastates mainframe if it reaches core.
  Sprite pulses red→white when HP < 30%.
  Counter: AOE resisted (they're bombs); PIERCE or KINETIC works best.

[GHOST] (stealth)
  hp: 50   speed: 1.8   damage: 2   50% invisChance
  resist: pierce 0.55×   weak: aoe 1.5×
  Phases in and out of detection.
  Counter: AOE reveals them (+50% dmg). Sniper wastes shots.

[JUGGERNAUT]
  hp: 280   speed: 0.6   damage: 4   armor: 14 (highest non-boss)
  resist: kinetic 0.15×, aoe 0.6×, pierce 0.7×   weak: energy 1.4×
  Heavily armored breach unit. Kinetic rounds barely scratch it. Energy melts it.
  Counter: ENERGY towers (QUANTUM, SCRAMBLER, PULSE, SENTINEL) deal full damage.

[PARASITE]                           ★ priority kill (renderer flags it)
  hp: 35   speed: 2.2 (FAST)   damage: 0
  resist: energy 0.6×   weak: aoe 1.2×
  Sacrifices itself to infect the nearest tower, halving its damage for 6s.
  Counter: kill before it reaches a tower. AOE and CHAIN can catch it mid-rush.

─── EXTREME THREAT ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[WRAITH]
  hp: 100   speed: 1.6   damage: 4   slow-immune
  resist: chain 0.35×, energy 0.8×
  Slow-immune. Shreds through chain attacks.
  Counter: burst damage (ICE / RAILGUN) beats DPS. Chain is weak.

─── BOSSES ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[KERNEL]                             threat: BOSS
  hp: 900   speed: 0.75   damage: 5   armor: 4   bossScale: yes
  resist: kinetic 0.7×, aoe 0.85×   weak: pierce 1.15×
  Core system boss. Massive HP pool.
  Counter: raw sustained DPS. PIERCE skips armor.

[DAEMON]                             threat: BOSS
  hp: 650   speed: 1.2   damage: 4   armor: 2   bossScale: yes
  resist: chain 0.7×   weak: energy 1.2×
  Spawns worms at 50% HP. Resists chain attacks.
  Counter: burst through 50% HP. ENERGY or KINETIC preferred.

[LEVIATHAN]                          threat: MEGA
  hp: 1800   speed: 0.6   damage: 8   armor: 6   slow-immune   bossScale: yes
  resist: aoe 0.6×, chain 0.4×   weak: pierce 1.2×
  Slow-immune. Regenerates. AOE and chain damped.
  Counter: single-target heavy hitters. RAILGUN + QUANTUM.

[SWARM QUEEN]                        threat: MEGA
  hp: 1200   speed: 0.9   damage: 6   bossScale: yes
  weak: aoe 1.4×, chain 1.2×
  Spawns a crawler every 1.5s. Weak to AOE.
  Counter: ICE-BREAKER + CHAIN shreds her and her brood.

[CORRUPTOR]                          threat: MEGA
  hp: 2200   speed: 0.7   damage: 10   armor: 8   bossScale: yes
  resist: chain 0× (IMMUNE), kinetic 0.85×, energy 0.9×
  Chain-immune. Corrupts tower signals nearby. Sprite shows scanline glitch band.
  Counter: skip CHAIN entirely. Stack KINETIC/PIERCE volume.

[VOID LORD]                          threat: FINAL ★ phase-shift mechanic
  hp: 4500   speed: 0.8   damage: 15   armor: 10   slow-immune   bossScale: yes
  resist (base): kinetic 0.6×, energy 0.65×, aoe 0.6×, chain 0.4×, pierce 0.7×
  CRIT IGNORES BASE RESIST (but NOT phase-shift immunity).
  Final threat. Phase-shifts damage immunity every 12s — watch the floating
  IMMUNE: <TYPE> tag. Body tints + ground aura match the active immunity color.
  Counter: multi-type coverage. Keep KINETIC + ENERGY + PIERCE all online so
  one rotation can never shut you down.
