Notation
  DMG  damage per shot        RATE  shots/second         TYPE  damage channel
  RNG  range in grid cells    HP    hit points           SPD   path speed
  [*]  rare-rarity card       [C]   capstone (last card in a branch)

Singleton rule: only 1 of each turret type per run. Branch keystones are
mutually exclusive — picking one locks out the other two for that turret.

KEY MECHANICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PORTS / EXPLOITS
  Every enemy has an "open port" (SSH/HTTP/DNS/SMB/ICMP). Turrets with a
  matching exploit deal +25% damage and pop an EXPLOIT! floater.
  Secondary layer orthogonal to damage-type resistances.

CLOAKING (invisTimer)
  GHOST PROTOCOL sector modifier / daily mutator spawns cloaked enemies.
  Single-target turrets (FIREWALL, ANTIVIRUS, QUANTUM, RAILGUN, SNIPER,
  PULSE, HONEYPOT, SENTINEL, SCRAMBLER) CANNOT target cloaked enemies.
  AOE and CHAIN turrets see through cloak AND reveal on hit (permanent).
  GHOST enemy flicker is a visual-only gimmick, not a targeting block.

SUBNET LINKS
  Adjacent turrets (≤1 cell, incl. diagonals) form a subnet.
    +8% damage per extra turret in subnet
    +12% damage per unique type in subnet
  Cap +60%. Diversity matters more than size.

OVERDRIVE ([O] hotkey on selected tower)
  4s @ 3× dmg / 2× rate → 6s offline → 30s recharge.
  Non-support turrets only. Best for boss waves or crowd surges.

HEAT BUILDUP (FIREWALL signature)
  Consecutive shots on same target +10% dmg (cap +50% at streak 6).
  Resets on target change.

PACKET DROPS
  8% of kills drop a packet (bosses always drop one). Tap within 5s:
    +D  pink   +50% tower dmg 8s
    +R  cyan   +50% fire rate 8s
    XP  green  instant +15 XP
    +H  yellow restore 5 integrity

PROGRAM DECK     removed — active abilities retired as broken.

ASCENSION / ICE DEPTH  (unlocks after any hard clear, max 10)
  Stacking global difficulty. Each level: +8% enemy HP, +3% speed;
  every 2 levels adds a turret lock; every 3 reduces draft size by 1.

NG+  (per-map tiers, max 5)
  First victory unlocks NG+1. Each victory at current tier raises max.
  Enemy HP +50%/tier, protocol reward +25%/tier.

DAILY CONTRACT  (seeded run, same for everyone per day)
  Unlocks after 3 map clears. Rolls a map + difficulty + mutator.
  GHOST PROTOCOL is gated behind owning at least one AOE/CHAIN turret.

SECTOR MODIFIERS  (always-on per-act effects)
  Act 1: no modifier (tutorial)
  Act 2: LAG SPIKE       — every 20s, all enemies surge at 2× speed for 1s
  Act 3: ENCRYPTED       — enemies spawn with HP shield (regen after 2s); cracking it EXPOSES the enemy for +25% DMG over 3s
  Act 4: STEALTH         — 15% spawns arrive cloaked
  Act 5: REPLICATION     — 15% of kills spawn a worm offspring
  Act 6: ROOTKIT JAM     — while a boss is alive, jam a random tower every 3s
  Act 7: OMEGA PROTOCOL  — all five modifiers at reduced strength
